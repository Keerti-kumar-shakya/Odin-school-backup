What are the types of clinical data?

There are five types of healthcare clinical data that are collected. These include the following categories:

  • administrative data
  • claims data
  • patient disease registries
  • health surveys
  • clinical trial data

What is medical data used for?

Medical data can help spot trends, recommend resources, aid in billing services, and help congruent patient care. It helps a physician understand

the wellbeing of the patient.

What does medical data include?

Meta data includes all the patient information about the client. This includes vitals, lab tests, assessments, referrals, and billing information.
